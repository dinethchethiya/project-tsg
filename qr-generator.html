free QR Generator

Create QR codes instantly in your browser. No upload, no backend.

Larger sizes create sharper downloads for banners, posters, and print use. Very large exports may take longer to render.

How it works

Enter text or a URL, click Generate, and download your QR as a PNG. Everything runs directly in your browser for quick creation without extra steps.

Privacy

Processed in browser, no uploads. Your input stays on your device, and no account is required.

Best for

  • Website and app links
  • Wi-Fi sharing cards
  • Restaurant or cafe menus
  • Event cards and invites

FAQ

Is this free?

Yes. You can generate and download QR codes for free.

Are my QR codes stored?

No. The generation happens client-side in your browser, and nothing is uploaded to a server.

Can I use this for business?

Yes. It works well for business use cases like menus, product links, campaigns, and event materials.
