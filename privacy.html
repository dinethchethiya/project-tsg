Privacy Policy

Most tools on this site run in your browser. This policy explains what data may still be processed for security, analytics, and advertising.

1) Controller and contact

This website is operated by thatsupportguy. For privacy questions, contact tsgbrandcontact@gmail.com.

2) Local processing of files

Core tool actions, including image conversion, PDF merge/compression, and QR generation, are designed to run locally in your browser. Uploaded source files are not intentionally sent to our server for core processing.

3) Data that may be processed

  • Technical request data such as IP address, browser type, and timestamps may be processed by hosting/CDN providers for security and delivery.
  • If enabled, analytics and advertising tags may process device identifiers, page views, and interaction events.
  • Google Fonts and CDN-hosted libraries may receive standard request metadata when assets are loaded.

4) Cookies, consent, and controls

Non-essential analytics and ad storage are denied by default until you opt in. You can update your choice at any time using the Privacy choices button shown on the site.

Depending on your region, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or limit processing of personal data. Contact us to submit a request.

5) Third-party services

  • Google AdSense / Google Ads / Google Analytics (if enabled)
  • Google Fonts
  • CDN providers used to serve JavaScript libraries

These providers operate under their own policies and may process data outside your country.

6) Retention and updates

We keep data only as long as needed for security, analytics, legal obligations, or service improvement. This policy may be updated as tools and integrations change.
